On March 4th, 2026, we hosted **Design Systems Breakfast** with [**Andy Cetnarskyj**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewcetnarskyj/)!

Andy walked us through **Supernova and how they use it to centralize design documentation and design tokens**—a practical overview of the tool and the features they rely on. Anyone curious about how teams structure docs and tokens in one place got a real-world perspective.

The session was relaxed and discussion-friendly. Designers, developers, and anyone involved in design systems joined in. We even stayed longer than the session was scheduled for, because the discussion was so good.
