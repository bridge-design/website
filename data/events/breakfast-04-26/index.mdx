Join us for our upcoming **Design Systems Breakfast** with
[**Igor**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/igor-gonak/), a Frontend Engineer specializing in design systems!

Igor works at **bonprix**, an international fashion e-commerce company,
where he has spent several years focused exclusively on their design system.
He will be presenting **"Beyond The Sandbox: Building a Bulletproof Testing
Suite with Storybook & Chromatic"** — a deep dive into turning familiar
tools into a comprehensive quality gate for your UI.

The talk will cover how to harness visual regression testing, interaction
tests, and automated workflows so your team can catch every UI bug before
it ships. If you're looking to build real confidence around design system
releases, this session will give you a concrete path to get there.

Our breakfasts are kept intentionally low-key and open to conversation.
Whether you're a designer, engineer, or somewhere in between, you're
welcome to pull up a chair, bring your coffee, and jump in.
