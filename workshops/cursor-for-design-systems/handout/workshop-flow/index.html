Workshop flow

Zoom spaces

In this workshop, you are working in cross-functional teams (designers and developers). We use Zoom's Main session for introduction and presentations that are adressed to both parties. For design- and development- related instructions we use Designers' Room and Developers' Room. For in-team communication, you can use your team's room (named as Team 1, Team 2, etc.)

Install zoom

https://zoom.us/download

Grant share screen permissions on mac

https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/360016688031-Using-the-Zoom-client-and-Zoom-Rooms-with-macOS

You can join another room freely any time, this way:

  1. Find a list of breakout rooms
  2. Use the "Join" button near the room's name. It might be visible on hover.

If you want to leave the room, you press blue button at the bottom right of the Zoom screen. Don't leave the meeting. If you accidentally left the meeting, join again. :-)

Host might invite you to a certain room. In this case you will see a popup about it.

Slack channels

Use GoRight Slack to exchange text messages. If you are not there yet, join by this link. In the slack, there is #hands-on-workshop## channel for our everyone's use and teams' channels as #team1, #team2, etc. All the channels are public, join the relevant team channel yourself.
